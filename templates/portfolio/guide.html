STEP-1 To Add a New Item!

STEP-2 Say, we want to add a new skill! Therefore, click on "Skills" in Navbar!

STEP-3 Click on Add Skill

STEP-4 Enter the details and then submit!

STEP-5 Click on "View Portfolio" to view how your portfolio would look like.

STEP-6 If you wish to update your "skill" details you entered previously. Navigate to the "skills" tab and click on "update"!

STEP-7 Swipe through the carousel to find the correct record, which you want to update and then click on "Click Here, If You Want To Update This Record!" button!

STEP-8 You can edit the details as per your choice and click on "update" to update your details. Your changes will be reflected to your portfolio.

STEP-9 You can view your portfolio again! The records will be updated!

STEP-10 Now if you wish to delete the "skill" record you entered previously. Navigate to the "skills" tab and click on "delete"!

STEP-11 Swipe through the carousel by clicking the left and right button, to get the record you want to delete and click "Delete" on the record you want to delete!

STEP-12 After you are done entering your portfolio details, you can download all your portfolio by clicking the "Download Portfolio" button!
